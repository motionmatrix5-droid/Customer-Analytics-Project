# Customer-Analytics-Project
A UK-based retail company selling home décor & gift items across multiple countries has thousands of SKUs, 4K+ customers, and daily invoice transactions. Leadership faces stagnant revenue, poor repeat purchase rates, and no visibility into customer profitability—blocking effective promotion, bundling, and inventory decisions
Outputs:

RFM & customer profitability segmentation
Most valuable customers
products with high demand
products with least demand
Product performance ranking and category insights
Actionable recommendations and playbooks to boost next-quarter revenue
Power BI dashboard for executive & operational users
